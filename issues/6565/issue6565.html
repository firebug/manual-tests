Issue 6565: Page mutations cause the Selectors side panel to scroll to the top

Steps to reproduce

  1. Open Firebug
  2. Switch to the CSS panel and there to the Selectors side panel
  3. Enter .test into the Try a selector... field and hit Enter
    ⇒ Ten <div>s should be listed inside the Selectors side panel.
  4. Resize the panel vertically, so that a vertical scrollbar appears
  5. Scroll the panel down
  6. Click the Create <div> button above

Observed result

  • The panel is completely refreshed and due to that scrolled up.

Expected result

  • Just the new <div> should be added to the panel and the scroll position shouldn't change.
