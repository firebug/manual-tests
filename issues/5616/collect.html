Disc Media

Media Supply is a great place to get:

  • Taiyo Yuden/JVC media, which works on all burners and players (no need to update firmeware) and comes at a good price. SHIPPING FREE usually on these if purchasing only taiyo-yuden/jvc products.
  • Gold and silver Archival media which lasts 300 or 250 years respectively
  • Disc duplication equipment and supplies
  • duplication services
  • Verbatim media, including their LightScribe media
  • BD-R discs in quantity and different speeds, and in SL and DL
  • large quantities of everything if you want it
  • jewel cases in bulk or singles
  • paper or tyvek sleeves in bulk
  • water-based cd pens (I always use a black ultra-fine point sharpie from any office supply store - they come in boxes too, not the color ones, they bleed through)
  • disc printers (some are merged with the duuplication units)
  • other stuff I can't remember offhand...
